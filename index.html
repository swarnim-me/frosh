About us

The primary task of Frosh is to ensure a hassle-free admission process for Freshers. The transition from school to college can be rough, but that's where Frosh comes in. Through a fun-packed series of electrifying events, interactive games, guest lectures, and cultural and technical workshops, it gives a kick start to the students’ social life. It also provides a platform for students to showcase their talents and emerge as confident individuals. This year, Frosh is prepared to break all preconceived notions about online events. The PROEM week features enthralling enigmas that take you on adventurous journeys from the safety of your homes, and engaging interactions with your peers that you thought you would miss out on. So, are you ready for this once in a lifetime experience?

Welcome aboard the Journey freshers. The journey to explore and experience the new you.
To Discover your Inner Self through us.

Our Team!

Our team picked up from different streams are like different beats coming together to complete the notes of a song.The mentors selected by the core after a tough round of interviews make an all rounder team.The Core members provide proper guidance to the mentors ,the mentors help the juniors to cope with the new lifestyle and atmosphere of the campus. Teachers are the main figures bridging the gap and imparting years of knowledge to core team to build new generation of leaders .With years of passing leadership skills, the team becomes a family and leads to healthy junior - senior relationship creating a chain of teaching and yet learning at the same time.The strength of our team is each member and strength of each member is our team and its our team work that makes our dream work.
